Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about my development services, technologies, and project approach. Don't see your question? Feel free to contact me directly.

1. What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in Flutter for cross-platform mobile development, Laravel for web applications, and React/MERN stack for modern web solutions. I'm also proficient with REST APIs, databases (MySQL, MongoDB, Firebase), and version control with Git.

2. What is your development process?

I follow a structured approach: requirement gathering, design planning, development, testing, and deployment. I maintain regular communication with clients, provide progress updates, and ensure the final product meets all specifications and quality standards.

3. Can you work on both frontend and backend?

Yes, I provide full-stack development services. I can handle frontend development with Flutter, React, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, as well as backend services using Laravel, API development, and database management to create complete end-to-end solutions.

4. How long do projects typically take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple projects may take a few weeks, while larger applications could take several months. I'll provide a detailed timeline estimate during the initial consultation after understanding your requirements.

5. Do you provide maintenance and support after project completion?

Yes, I offer post-launch maintenance and support services. This includes bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and technical support to ensure your application continues to run smoothly and securely.
